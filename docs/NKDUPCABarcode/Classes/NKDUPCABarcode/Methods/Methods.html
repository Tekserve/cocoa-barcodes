generateChecksum

Abstract: Overridden to generate UPC-A check digit 
- (void)generateChecksum;

The algorithm is as follows, which is a slightly more complex modulus 10 algorithm:

Designate the rightmost character odd. Sum all of the characters in the odd positions and multiply the result by three. Sum all of the characters in the even positions Add the odd and even totals from steps two and three. Determine the smallest number that when added to the result from step four, will result in a multiple of 10. This is the check character.
