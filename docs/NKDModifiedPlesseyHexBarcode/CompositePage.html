NKDModifiedPlesseyHexBarcode.h


Discussion



This is an extension of the Plessey code. The Plessey specification sets out the encoding scheme for numeric data, but leaves the other bar combinations open. This is the most common use of the open combinations - to encode the letters A through F, allowing the use of Hex notation to represent 0-255 in a two character combination.

THIS IS NOT GUARANTEED TO PRODUCE A BARCODE THAT IS IN COMPLIANCE WITH THE SPECIFICATION.
