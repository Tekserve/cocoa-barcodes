NKDPostnetBarcode.h


Discussion



This is a concrete subclass of NKDBarcode that implements a two of five barcode that uses the height of the bars, rather than the width, to encode numeric data according to US Postal Service specifications.

We are using a bar width of .015" and a gap space of .030" - this spacing is within the US Postal Service's specs and since the gap space is exactly twice the bar width, we can represent the gap with two zeros and represent all bars as one one. The real logic for this barcode is in the barTop method, which makes it a somewhat unusual subclass of NKDBarcode.

#defines

SHORT_BAR

#define SHORT_BAR .05

Defines the height, according to USPS specification, of a short bar

TALL_BAR

#define TALL_BAR .125

Defines the height, according to USPS specification, of a tall bar
