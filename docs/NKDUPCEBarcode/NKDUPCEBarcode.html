NKDUPCEBarcode.h



This subclass implements the abbreviated form of the UPC symbol, the UPC-E which reduces the 12 characters of the UPC-A down to 8 (six characters, check digit encoded in parity of other digits). It is a subclass of NKDUPCABarcode beccause UPC-E does not have it's own check digit algorithm, rather the UPC-A check digit (calculated on all 12 digits of the original value) and number system are encoded in the parity order of the other six digits.
